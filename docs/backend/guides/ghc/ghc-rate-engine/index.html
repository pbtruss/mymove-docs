GHC Rate Engine

The Rate Engine is the tool that determines what the Prime will get paid for various components of their move.

We rely on the Rate Engine to price moves according to the most current pricing template.

The information from the pricing template has been broken down to show what inputs are needed for pricing service items.

There is a design doc on how to price a service item.
